Park Design in Mărăști District

Initiative Information
Urban Planning Expires in 5 days

This initiative aims to redesign the park in the Mărăști district to better serve the community's needs. The park has been in need of renovation for several years, and the city has allocated funds for this project. Three design proposals have been developed by different architectural firms, and citizens are invited to vote for their preferred design.

The winning design will be implemented starting next spring, with an estimated completion time of 6 months. Your participation in this vote is crucial for ensuring that the park meets the community's expectations and needs.
